Recruitment at Tactical Africa

TacticalAfrica only employ former government protection, surveillance and intelligence personnel via an internal selection process. If you are interested in working with us then please fill in this form. We pride ourselves on our standards of service delivery and this also applies to our recruitment processes. If you meet our recruitment requirements and do not receive a reply from us within 5 working days then please do follow up.

Due to the secretive nature of our operations applicants may be subjected to extensive vetting. Tactical Africa Ltd reserves the right to vet all applicants for employment. Tactical Africa reserves the right to refuse any application without providing reason. Also, please bear in mind that whilst you may have the requisite career background, this does not automatically qualify you to work with us.
